Why Pitch & Hitch

Built different.
By design.

Friend-Powered Pitches
Your best friend is your hype person. They take the stage and pitch you using a short, funny slide deck that actually shows your personality.
Curated Crowd
Not a sea of strangers — a carefully sized room of people who actually showed up to connect. Quality over quantity, every time.
Mutual-Match Reveal
You "spark" someone quietly during the night. Matches only reveal at the end — eliminating rejection anxiety and keeping the magic alive.
Zero Pressure Atmosphere
Group energy, live Q&A, laughter, and free-flowing mingling mean you never feel put on the spot. The room does the work.
Theme-Driven Nights
Every event has its own flavour — PowerPoint & Pinot, Rom-Com Chaos, Soft Launch Singles. Each night is completely different.
Live Gamification
Audience polls, reaction boards, and leaderboards keep energy high and make the whole night feel like a shared event — not just a date attempt.
