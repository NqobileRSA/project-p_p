Why Pitch & Hitch

Built different.
By design.

Friend-Powered PowerPoint Pitches
Your best friend is your hype person. They take the stage with a short, fun slide deck that captures your personality — the real you, not a filtered bio.
For Singles & Wingpeople
Come as the single being pitched, the friend doing the pitching, or just someone open to meeting new people. Every role is welcome here.
Respect Comes Standard
Every pitch should be fun, kind and consent-based. Pitch & Hitch is about celebrating people — not embarrassing them. Good energy only.
Low Stakes, High Energy
Group energy, live Q&A, laughter, and free-flowing mingling mean you never feel put on the spot. Even if romance doesn't happen, you'll leave with new friends.
Rooms Built for Connection
Not a sea of strangers — a carefully sized room of people who actually showed up to connect. Quality over quantity, every time.
Johannesburg's Fresh Alternative
Not anti-app — just pro-real-life. A different option for people who want to meet someone in a room, with laughter, in a city that's ready for it.
The Night

Four moves.
One unforgettable night.

01

Arrive

Check in, grab a drink, settle into a curated room of real, open, social singles — and the friends who've got their backs.

02

Pitch

Friends take the stage with a short PowerPoint. Fun, warm, and genuinely you — pitches are designed to celebrate, not embarrass.

03

Mingle

Q&A, laughter, group energy. Then free mingling — no timed rounds, no awkward buzzers. Just good conversation and real people.

04

Connect

If there's a spark, follow it. Pitchers may share contact details or social handles after the event. The pressure is off — the possibility is on.
